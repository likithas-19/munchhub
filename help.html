Frequently Asked Questions

The Budget Optimizer uses a customized **Knapsack dynamic programming algorithm**. When you enter your exact pocket budget, it scans all available food items across campus cafes, calculates the maximum possible rating and nutritional content, and builds the single optimal combination of dishes to fit your exact budget limit.

When you place an order, our engine solves the shortest delivery route using **Dijkstra's shortest path algorithm**. If you enter an off-campus home address, a new vertex is dynamically injected into the campus graph at runtime. It automatically connects to the nearest exit boundary terminal using GPS-scaled coordinate distances to determine the fastest path out of campus.

Group Ordering allows you and your roommates to create a shared order room. The system aggregates all member items into a single payment list. Then, using a custom graph geometric median calculator, it determines the central shortest common pickup point to split the walking delivery distance equally among everyone.
